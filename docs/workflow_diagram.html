Methodology Workflow
LULC Change Detection and Markov Chain Prediction in Dhaka District
using Google Earth Engine
1 Study Area Definition – Dhaka District
↓
2 Data Acquisition – Landsat 8 OLI (2015) & Landsat 9 OLI (2025), USGS Collection 2 Level 2
↓
3 Preprocessing – Cloud masking (QA_PIXEL), radiometric scaling, annual median composite
↓
4 Training Data Collection – Digitized samples (Water, Built-up, Green area)
↓
5 CART Classification – 6 spectral bands, 70:30 train/test split
↓
6 Accuracy Assessment – Confusion matrix, OA 95.9%, Kappa 0.93
↓
7 Classified LULC Maps – 2015 and 2025
↓
8 Post-Classification Change Detection & Area Calculation
↓
9 Markov Chain Modeling – Pixel-by-pixel transition probability matrix (2015→2025)
↓
10 Future Prediction – 2035 LULC map (business-as-usual scenario)
↓
11 Outputs – Maps, tables, bar charts
